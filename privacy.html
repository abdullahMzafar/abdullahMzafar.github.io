Overview

This website is a personal portfolio hosted on GitHub Pages. We respect your privacy and aim to collect only what is necessary to understand site performance and protect our contact form from spam.

Analytics (Google Analytics 4 via Google Tag Manager)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) through Google Tag Manager (GTM) to measure visits and interactions. The site implements Google Consent Mode:

  • Before consent: GA4 operates in a limited, cookieless mode (aggregated measurements with reduced granularity).
  • After “Accept”: GA4 uses cookies to provide full analytics.

No personally identifiable information is collected unless you voluntarily submit it via the acceptance acknowledgement.

For details, see Google’s policies: https://policies.google.com/privacyGoogle Privacy Policy and https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookiesCookies.

Contact Form & reCAPTCHA

To prevent spam, the contact form uses Google reCAPTCHA v2, which may collect device and interaction data to distinguish humans from bots. This is considered strictly necessary for security.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacyGoogle Privacy Policy and https://www.google.com/recaptcha/about/About reCAPTCHA.

Cookies

Non-essential analytics cookies are used only after you accept via the consent banner. You can change your decision by clearing site data in your browser.

Your choices

  • Use the consent banner to accept or reject analytics cookies.
  • Manage or delete cookies using your browser settings.
